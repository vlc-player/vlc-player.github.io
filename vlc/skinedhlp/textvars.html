Text Variables

These variables will be replaced in Text items and in the tooltiptexts of other items.

  • $N - Name of the currently played item
  • $F - Full name (with path) of the currently played item
  • $V - Value of the volume in percent
  • $T - Current time of playback (format: H:MM:SS)
  • $L - Remaining time of playback (format: H:MM:SS)
  • $D - Total duration of the current item of playback (format: H:MM:SS)
  • $t, $l, $d - Same as $T, $D, $L, except that the hour is only shown when it's not 0.
  • $H - Help text. Defined by the help attribute of the item over which the mouse is currently.
  • $B - The audio stream bitrate in kb/s
  • $S - The audio stream sample rate in kHz
