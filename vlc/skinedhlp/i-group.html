Group

A simple container for elements, that groups them together and thus makes it easier for the skin designer to move several items at once. As of VLC 0.9.0 Groups are deprecated and replaced by the more powerful Panel item.

General Attributes

X and Y
The coordinates of the item relative to the parent container (Layout, Panel or Group).
